‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I find my local mayor or governor?
FEC Campaign Tracker only shows federal elections (President, Senate, House of Representatives). State and local candidates are tracked by different agencies and aren't included in federal data.
How often is the data updated?
Data comes directly from the Federal Election Commission and is updated as candidates file their reports. Most active candidates file monthly or quarterly.
How accurate is this data?
All data comes directly from official Federal Election Commission records, so it's as accurate as the government's own records. We don't modify or interpret the data - we just present it in an easy-to-understand format.
How does search history work?
The app automatically saves your last 20 searches with all filters applied. You can access them via the clock icon next to the search bar, delete individual items by swiping, or clear all history at once.
Does dark mode save battery?
Yes! On OLED displays (iPhone X and newer), dark mode can significantly reduce battery consumption since black pixels don't use power. It also reduces eye strain in low-light conditions.
Can I export candidate data?
Yes! Tap the export button on any candidate's detail page to save, print, email, or text their complete contribution data. The export includes all contributors, not just the top ones.
Is this app free?
Yes! FEC Campaign Tracker is completely free with no ads. We believe campaign finance transparency should be accessible to everyone. Optional tips are available to support development.

üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

App Won't Load Data

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try force-closing and reopening the app
  • Restart your device if the problem persists

Search Results Not Showing

  • Make sure you're searching for federal candidates only
  • Try searching with just the last name
  • Check your filters - they might be too restrictive

Export Not Working

  • Ensure the Files app is enabled on your device
  • Check that you have permission to save files
  • Try a different export method if one fails
