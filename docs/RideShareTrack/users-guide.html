🎯 Getting Started

First Time Setup

  • Grant location permission for automatic mileage tracking
  • Allow camera access for receipt scanning
  • Enable voice commands for hands-free categorization
  • Review privacy settings - all data stays on your device
  • Consider upgrading to Tax Pro for full automation

Data Safety

  • All your data is stored locally on your device
  • Upgrading to Tax Pro preserves all existing trips and expenses
  • No data loss when switching between Free and Pro tiers

App Organization

  • Tracker Tab: Start/stop mileage tracking
  • Expenses Tab: Add business expenses & view receipts
  • Trips Tab: Review and categorize drives
  • Reports Tab: Generate tax documents
  • Help Tab: Access this guide and support

🚗 Tracking Trips

Automatic Tracking (Tax Pro)

  • App detects when you start/stop driving
  • Automatically calculates mileage
  • Voice commands for hands-free categorization
  • Background tracking enabled

Manual Tracking (Free)

  • Tap "Start Tracking" when beginning rides
  • Tap "Stop Tracking" when finished
  • App calculates distance automatically
  • Set trip category (business/personal)

Voice Commands (Tax Pro)

  • Say "Business" when starting a passenger trip
  • Say "Personal" when driving for yourself
  • App speaks: "Starting trip. Is this business or personal?"
  • Works even with gloves or while driving
💡 Important: Business trips are tax-deductible! Personal trips aren't.

💰 Managing Expenses

Common Deductible Expenses

  • Gas and fuel
  • Car maintenance and repairs
  • Insurance premiums
  • Registration fees
  • Tolls and parking
  • Cell phone (business use)

Receipt Scanning (Tax Pro)

  • Take photo of receipt
  • App extracts amount and date
  • Review and categorize
  • Automatically saves as deductible
  • View all receipts in gallery

Receipt Gallery

  • Browse all saved receipt photos
  • Search receipts by description or category
  • Filter by expense type (Gas, Maintenance, etc.)
  • View full-screen receipt details
  • Share receipt images for tax preparation
⚠️ Important: Keep original receipts for your records!

📋 Tax Advice

IRS Tax Guidelines

  • 2025 Mileage Rate: $0.70/mile
  • Standard Mileage Deduction includes:
    • Depreciation
    • Fuel
    • Insurance
    • Registration
    • Repairs

Important Rules

  • Separate business and personal trips
  • Keep detailed records
  • Mileage deduction OR actual expenses
  • Don't double-deduct
⚠️ Important: Consult a tax professional for specific advice!

🔧 Troubleshooting

❌ Location not working

✅ Check Location Services in Settings, ensure app has permission

❌ Camera not scanning receipts

✅ Grant camera permission, ensure good lighting, hold steady

❌ Tracking stopped unexpectedly

✅ Disable battery optimization for the app in Settings

❌ Data disappeared

✅ Data stays on device - check if you logged into different iCloud account

❌ Subscription not working

✅ Check App Store receipt, restart app, contact support

🔧 Still having issues? Contact support through the app!
